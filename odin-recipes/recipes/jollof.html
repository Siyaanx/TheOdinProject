African Jollof Rice with Chicken Recipe

Description

This is the ultimate recipe for 3-4 servings of jollof rice with chicken that taste like home in Africa.
Jollof rice is prepared with the Chicken stocks to give a savoury chicken arroma and taste.
The Full meal can be served with Plantain, Fish, Stew, and Salad.

Chicken

Steps

  • Step 1

    1. Place chicken drumsticks after rinsing properly in a large pot.
    2. Add 1/2 onion, ginger, crushed bouillon cubes, garlic, curry powder, 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
    3. Pour in water, mix very well and place over stove.
    4. Cook until chicken starts sticking to the bottom, about 15 minutes.
    5. Pour in little water, cover the pot, and bring to a gentle simmer; cook for 5 minutes. Remove heat.

  • Step 2

    1. Transfer chicken to a baking dish using a slotted spoon.
    2. Strain cooking liquid through a fine-mesh sieve.
    3. Reserve 1 1/2 cups liquid. Discard solids.
